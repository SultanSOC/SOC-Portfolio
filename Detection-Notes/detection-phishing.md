# Detection Note: Phishing Email Detection

## 1. Overview
Phishing attacks attempt to trick users into revealing sensitive information or executing malicious content.

---

## 2. Why It Matters
Can lead to credential theft or malware infection.

---

## 3. Key Indicators
- Suspicious sender email
- Urgent language
- Malicious or disguised links
- Unexpected attachments

---

## 4. Detection Logic
Analyze email headers, links, and attachments. Check if user interacted with the email.

---

## 5. Example Scenario
User receives email with fake login page link.

---

## 6. False Positives
- Legitimate marketing emails

---

## 7. Conclusion
Suspicious email behavior should always be investigated carefully.
